## 2. Project Overview

The Preschool Management System includes four main platforms:

1. **Admin Dashboard** - School management and operations
2. **Teacher Dashboard** - Class management and student tracking
3. **Parent/Client Dashboard** - Parent notifications and updates
4. **Public Website** - Marketing and admissions (Charged Separately)
